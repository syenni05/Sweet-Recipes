Small Batch Chocolate Buttercream Cupcakes Recipe

{{item.content}}

Ingredients

For the cupcakes:

- 1/4 cup boiling water

- 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa

- 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour

- 3/4 teaspoon baking powder

- 1/8 teaspoon salt

- 6 tablespoons sugar

- 1 Half Stick (1/4 cup) Land O Lakes® Butter, softened

- 1 large Land O Lakes® Egg

- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the buttercream:

- 1 Half Stick (1/4 cup) Land O Lakes® Butter, softened

- 1 cup powdered sugar

- 1/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa

- Dash salt

- 1/2 ounce bittersweet chocolate, melted

- 2 tablespoons Land O Lakes® Heavy Whipping Cream

Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375°F. Place paper baking cups into muffin pan cups or grease cups; set aside.

2. Place boiling water and 3 tablespoons cocoa into bowl; mix well. Let stand until slightly cooled.

3. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in another bowl; set aside.

4. Place sugar and 1/4 cup butter into bowl; beat at medium speed until creamy. Add egg; beat until well mixed. Stir in vanilla. Add flour mixture; beat at low speed just until combined. Add cocoa mixture; beat until well mixed.

5. Spoon batter evenly into prepared muffin pan cups. Bake 18-22 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool completely.

6. Beat 1/4 cup butter in bowl until creamy. Beat at low speed, gradually adding powdered sugar, 1/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa and salt. Add melted chocolate and heavy whipping cream; beat until mixed.

7. Frost or pipe buttercream onto cooled cupcakes.
