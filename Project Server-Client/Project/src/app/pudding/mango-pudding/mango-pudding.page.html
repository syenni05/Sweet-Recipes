The Mango Pudding Recipe

This recipe is the Chinese style mango pudding popular in Dim Sum store.

{{item.content}}

Ingredients

For the puree mixture:

- 150 g mango puree

- 60 ml cold water

- 20 ml Heavy cream

- 40 ml milk

For the gelatin mixture:

- 8 g gelatin, (2% of the total weight)

- 60 g sugar

- 80 ml water

For garnish:

- Mango cubes

- Coconut milk

- Fresh mint leaves

Instructions

1. Cut the mango into the half. Use a knife to cut criss-cross on the flesh to make some dices.

2. Keep it for the subsequent step.

3. Remove the remaining flesh from the skin and the pit.

4. Place the mango flesh in the blender. Add the water as in the recipe. Blend the mango until it becomes a smooth puree.

5. Pour the pureed mango through a wire mesh strainer. Use the back of a metal spoon to press and stir lightly so that the puree will pass through the strainer.

6. Add the cream and milk to the puree.

7. Put cold water in a small pan.

8. Dissolve the gelatin in the cold water.

9. Let it sits for five minutes to allow it to bloom.

10. Now add the sugar into the Bloomed gelatin. Heat it slowly until the sugar dissolved. Remove from heat.

11. Pour the gelatin mixture slowly into the mango puree mixture.

12. Pour it through the strainer for the second time.

13. Place some mango cubes in the cup.

14. Fill up the molds with the mixture.

15. Use the tip of a folded kitchen tissue to break the bubbles.

16. Cover with cling film and chill for at least two hours or until the pudding is set.

17. Add a teaspoon of milk (or coconut milk) on the pudding.

18. Decorate with more mango cubes and mint leaves.
