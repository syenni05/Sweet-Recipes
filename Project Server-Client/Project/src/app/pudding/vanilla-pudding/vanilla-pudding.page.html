Basic Vanilla Pudding Recipe

{{item.content}}

Ingredients

- 3 cups milk (divided)

- 1/4 cup cornstarch

- 1/2 cup sugar

- 1/4 teaspoon salt

- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Scald 2 and 2/3 cups of the milk.

2. In a small bowl, mix together the cornstarch, sugar, and salt.

3. Stir in the remaining 1/3 cup milk.

4. Add to the scalded milk and cook over low heat, constantly stirring, until thickened and smooth.

5. Continue cooking for about 5 minutes to cook cornstarch thoroughly.

6. Cool slightly and then stir in vanilla.

7. Pour into serving dishes.
